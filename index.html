5 things you do on Facebook that you'd never do in real life.

Ralph Jones - 30 January 2015






It can be tempting, as 'real life' and digital technologies become ever more intertwined, to be fooled into thinking that Facebook = real life. But Facebook does not = real life. There are crucial differences between the two. Because some activities can be hidden on Facebook more than they could be in real life, and because some can be made public more easily, we project a very specific type of image about ourselves on the social media website. There are things you pretend to like that you are in fact indifferent to; there are pressures to which you succumb because you'd feel too awkward not to; and then of course there's all the bikini photos. So enjoy these, the 12 things you do on Facebook that you'd never do in real life. Then sign out and go frolic in the snow or something.

Say you'll definitely go to events you have no intention of attending

5

Track down that kid you knew when you were five years old to see what he's up to nowt
